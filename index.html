📋– SPIRITUAL GUIDE

1. Buying A Book
Open the Book Store and choose the book you want.

2. M-Pesa Payment
Pay using the M-Pesa number displayed under the selected book package.

3. Receiving Your License Key
After payment is confirmed, a license key is sent to the phone number used during payment.

4. Unlocking A Book
Press UNLOCK, enter your license key, then press Unlock Book.

5. Reading Your Book
After successful verification, the book opens instantly and can be accessed from your library.

6. Rekindle View
If a page becomes blank, freezes, loads incorrectly, or the reading view is interrupted, press ⚡ REKINDLE VIEW. The system will refresh and restore the current book page automatically without affecting your unlocked access.

7. Upgrade When Limit Is Reached
If you reach the package page limit, press Upgrade Required and select the next package.

8. Upgrade Payment
Pay the upgrade package through the displayed M-Pesa number and wait for your new upgrade key.

9. Upgrade Activation
Enter the upgrade key and press Activate Upgrade to continue reading.

10. Premium Package
Premium unlocks the complete book without page restrictions.

11. Support
If a payment or key issue occurs, contact the administrator for assistance.
